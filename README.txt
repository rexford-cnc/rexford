Rexford static site (ES default, EN toggle)
===========================================

Upload your images into the /assets folder using these exact lowercase filenames:

- rexford-logo.png          (header & footer logo)
- ffg.png
- expand.png
- fanuc.png
- mitsubishi.png
- ecoroll.png
- kennametal.png
- hainbuch.png
- calibratools.png
- allied.png
- rexfordcarbide.png
- hero.jpg   (optional hero image)

How to preview locally
----------------------
Double-click index.html (Chrome recommended). No server needed.

Deploy tips
-----------
Drag & drop the entire rexford-site/ folder (not just index.html) into your host.
